Know Your Attitude

Though the types of relationships we see in movies are not necessarily true in real life, some people continue to live in fantasy land and pattern their relationships after movie situations too much, especially women.

However, some women might not aware their attitude and expectations are unrealistic. We start off with testing the attitude that shows how much a woman could differentiate between shows and reality.
